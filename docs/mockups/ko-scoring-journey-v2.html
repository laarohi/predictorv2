Variant 1 · recommended
Round bars — one bar per round

One proportional bar per tournament round. Green = banked, gold = still in play. Length = points, so the bar carries the meaning — and because early rounds are played and late rounds aren't, the green-to-gold gradient is your journey, with zero rail scaffolding. The headline band splits your KO points by source (⚽ match vs ◆ bracket) so match points finally have a home. Tap a row → the fixtures & teams behind that bar.
